Proud to be...
Welcome to Rhodesian Ridgeback Rescue, Inc.

Since its beginnings in 1970, Rhodesian Ridgeback Rescue, Inc., (RRRInc) has been dedicated to the rescue, care and adoption of Rhodesian Ridgeback dogs in need. RRRInc now operates throughout the USA and Canada. The purpose of this site is to...
  • Seek adoptive homes for rescued Ridgebacks
  • Provide education about rescue and adoption
  • Distribute information about the Rhodesian Ridgeback breed
Let Us Help You...
  • Learn About Dogs Available for Adoption in the USA and Canada
  • Contact Rescue Coordinators in Your Area
  • Learn Things to Know Before Purchasing a Ridgeback Puppy
We recommend that you review our What is a Ridgeback?, What is Rescue?, and Adopt a Ridgeback pages prior to considering an adoption. If you are not interested in adopting a Ridgeback but would like to assist Rhodesian Ridgeback Rescue, Inc., in its mission, please see our How You Can Help page.
